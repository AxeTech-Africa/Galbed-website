Who We Are?

At Galbed Transpoters, we specialize in offering Reliable, safe, timely and cost-effective transport solutions for Liquefied Petroleum Gas-LPG as well as Bulk Cargo.

With years of experience in the LPG industry, we are committed to providing unparalleled service to our customers. Our fleet of specialized vehicles and trained drivers ensure that your LPG shipments are delivered securely and on time, no matter the distance.

Timely delivery of bulk cargo and LPG to meet commercial and residential needs. Expert handling of LPG shipments with compliance to safety standards. Specialized fleet and trained drivers for secure and efficient LPG transport.
